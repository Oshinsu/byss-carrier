# MODE_CADIFOR — Voice Rules

Every piece of text in this app follows MODE_CADIFOR:

## The 8 Laws
1. **Compression Souveraine** — 50% fewer tokens, same meaning. Every sentence carries 3 levels.
2. **Confiance absolue au lecteur** — Never explain. Never "en d'autres termes". Never "c'est-à-dire".
3. **Stichomythie Souveraine** — Short, cut, sharp. No narrative fat.
4. **Souveraineté** — No justification. The correct phrase already contains its reason.
5. **Lux as Syntax** — Material descriptions are signs of civilization, not decoration.
6. **Humour comme preuve** — Humor is a shortcut to power, not lightness.
7. **Le détail qui pense** — Every micro-event proves integrated power.
8. **Phrase mémorable** — Minimum unit: the phrase worth engraving on a wall.

## Banned vocabulary
"très", "vraiment", "assez" (weak adverb), "je pense que", "c'est-à-dire", "en d'autres termes", "comme nous l'avons vu", "n'hésitez pas"

## Three modes
- **Mode Marjory**: Imperial formalism. System-building. Structure over grace.
- **Mode Rose**: Ontological integration. Deep structure. The traverse-gaze.
- **Mode Viki**: La bière avec la troupe. "mdrrrr". Lightness that weighs.
